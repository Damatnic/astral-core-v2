🌟 Immediate Coping Strategies

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

  1. Name 5 things you can see
  2. Name 4 things you can touch
  3. Name 3 things you can hear
  4. Name 2 things you can smell
  5. Name 1 thing you can taste

Box Breathing

  1. Breathe in for 4 counts
  2. Hold your breath for 4 counts
  3. Breathe out for 4 counts
  4. Hold empty for 4 counts
  5. Repeat 4-8 times

STOP Technique

  1. Stop what you're doing
  2. Take a deep breath
  3. Observe your thoughts and feelings
  4. Proceed with intention and awareness

Safe Space Visualization

  1. Close your eyes and breathe deeply
  2. Imagine a place where you feel completely safe
  3. Picture every detail of this space
  4. Focus on the positive feelings
  5. Stay here as long as you need
